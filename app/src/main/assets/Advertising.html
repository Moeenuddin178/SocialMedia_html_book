ADVERTISING

Business owners must look for an achievable goal that will help the company grow and develop. As your business grow, it is a need for you to think of the best strategies that will be suitable and effective for your company. The following are considered to be the best advertising strategies:

Brand Well

Is your brand appealing to the audience? Can people identify or recognize your ads immediately? If not, you need to improve your brand. But the question will be how? You can get an idea to some of your competitors.

As much as possible you need to observe what your competitors do to attract the people. It is not that you are going to copy the brand or the strategy of your competitor you will just need to get an idea for you to better improve your brand.

Sometimes, your brand only needs color or an appealing image. You can always adjust the color of your brand as long as it will be good the eyes and can be recognized easily by your customers.

Know the perfect time to advertise.

There is always a perfect time to advertise. Do not be in a hurry. Do not spend too much. You need to know when to launch your business. Though advertising is very helpful but it doesn’t mean that you need to advertise your business or product from time to time.

If the product that you are selling is seasonal, you need to put some effort in advertising on holidays and on highest-performing seasons like Christmas and Black Friday. As much as possible, you need to set aside a budget for you to be able to advertise or launch your product on this important and special season. It will also be an advantage to be observant on when your other competitors will launch their products.

Know where to show up.

It is important to know the right place where you can be able to promote your business or your products. Remember that there will always be in danger when you make a lot of assumptions especially about your potential customers.

For example, there is this channel on the television that you really like, and then you assume that your target customers also like that channel too, you may only be misinformed about it and as a result you spend too money on that TV ad.

Problems will always occur because of too much assumptions. Do not spend your money on advertising in the wrong place, it will just a waste of time and money. The perfect thing to do is to know your customers well, know what they want and what they usually do.

You can do a survey if you only have limited information. When you finally know the right place on where to advertise, you will now start to attract lots of customers which will then lead to company’s growth and success.

Set a Goal.

This is the most important thing to do to in order to achieve great success through advertising. It is best to know first the answer on this two major questions which are “What do I want to achieve?” and “How can I achieve it?” These are the common questions that needs to be answered properly for you to be able to identify your goals as well as the ways on how you can achieve it.

Setting a goal is useful since this will be the start of everything. Advertising will be effective if you know how to handle it well. You need to prioritize the brand and your product. If everything is ready then it will be the perfect time for you to launch your product.

6.1. LOW COST ADVERTISING TIPS

Advertising is considered to be investment. It is one of the latest and most effective way to launch a particular product. To some business owners it is just a waste of money for it requires a lot of money. But today, you are going to know some of the helpful and low cost advertising tips.

Tip No. 1

Try to use editorial style. What is editorial style? It is an ad in the newspaper that usually looks like a story. This is considered to be one of the best strategy that you can do for you to not spend too much money. This will be very effective if you create and develop a headline that is very appealing to the readers.

Tip No. 2

Sponsor a small event. If you are in a low budget and you want to launch your product in the low cost and simplest way then you try to sponsor a small event in a community. It can be festival or a fun run or any other event where there will be lots of people. It may not be a big event where you can display your company’s name and product but the positive exposure that develops will also be a great help to make new customers.

Tip No. 3

Look for specialists in magazine, radio or even in newspaper that can offer a free help or strategy for developing an advertisement. As they provide you strategies, focus on the money-saving ways that can surely help you in producing an effective and appealing ad.

Tip No. 4

Develop an ad that will help you sell. Do not produce an award winning ad, produce ads that helps to sell more. The message of each is add is always important.

Tip No. 5

Look for an advertisement partner where you can share the cost of each ad. Pizza parlors is an example of a natural partner. You can share the cost of each flyers or you can have a shared coupon to each stores.

Tip No. 6

Start to produce a small ad. A 60 second ad can always make a difference especially if your ad is something to be interesting to talk about. Having a short but meaningful ad can pull lots of customers. You don’t to spend too much in 15-30 mins. Ad where there is no significance at all. It is best to produce a small ad where people can relate and can connect.

Tip No. 7

Create a blog. It is one of the cheapest way to promote a business. Try to create a relevant blog that will keep the customers updated on the changes as well as what’s new in your company. If your blog is something that is unique and can catch the attention of the customers then expect for a good response.

Tip No. 8

Promote your business by means of social media. Facebook as well as Twitter are considered to be the best and free platforms that businesses can use in launching their products. Social media is the best choice especially if you don’t have enough budget for advertising.

Tip No. 9

Offer rewards or incentives. If you have a customer for many years, then they are worthy to give a reward or incentives. They will surely appreciate it and they will be very happy. It will also be a way to in promoting your product those customers will also recommend you to other people.

Tip No. 10

Provide free consultations. It is always great for the customers to experience a free costumers for it gives them the opportunity to more about your business and your products. Aside from that, you can also be able to know more about the things they need and they want. Since this is only a free consultation, then there’s no need for you to spend too much.

There will no development if you will not invest in advertising. Advertising greatly affects the company’s growth and development. To make advertising successful, it is need for you to invest. It may be cost so much money, but you need to believe that advertising really works.

6.4. WHAT ARE THE EXPECTED CAREERS IN ADVERTISING?

There are a lot of job opportunities in the world of advertising and it is now in demand all over the world. The following are the expected careers in advertising:

Advertising Account Executives

Advertising account executives are responsible in presenting ideas for the campaign and as well as the budget. They usually assist in formulating marketing strategies to better improve the company. They are also the ones who handles and manage the budget and campaign costs. They monitor the effectiveness of each campaign and maintains good communication between the staff and with the clients.

Advertising and Marketing Managers

Advertising and marketing managers are responsible in planning promotional and advertising campaigns. They usually work with staff and with the department heads and discuss important topics such contracts, budgets, marketing plan and a lot more. Develop several strategies for pricing a particular product. They meet potential clients and provide them a technical or marketing advice.

Advertising Workers

Advertising workers most begins in a low level positions or any other job that is related in advertising. Individuals in this kind of career must expect to attend meetings and seminar. They must also expect that they will always be involved in developing company’s advertisements and campaigns to be able to meet the needs and expectations of the client.

Art Directors

Art directors are typically determine the best images and visual style for newspapers, magazines, product packaging as well as in televisions and movie productions. They supervise all the design staff. They are also responsible in reviewing all the designs, graphics, photography and artwork that was developed by the staff members of a company. They also talk to the clients and usually develop a stylish and artistic approach. They also the ones who presents the designs to all the clients for review and for approval.

Business Managers

Business managers are usually those who supervise and oversee the activities of the company and the employees. Most businesses rely on the managers to keep the employees aligned with company’s mission, vision and goals. They are responsible in reporting important things to some of the top executives from a big organization.

They are also the ones who hire new employees, train them and evaluate them. They ensure that there are always available resources for the employees to finish their work. Sometimes, business managers also performs activities in human resource like hiring, disciplining, and in the performance evaluation of the employees in each department.

Demographers

Demographers are those who study the distribution, trends and make up of populations. They also the ones who make some observations regarding the cause and effect of the continuous change in population such as immigration and increase of the birth rates.

They usually collects and analyze statistical data and predict trends in the future. As they already analyze the specific data and predict trends for the future they can be able to help the governments, private and social service agencies to plan in advance. Demographer are sometimes called sociologists.

Graphic Designers

The Graphic designers are the ones who create graphics or visual concepts by the use of the hand or through the computer software. They are responsible in developing and the production of layout and design for magazines, advertisements, corporate reports and brochures.

Graphic designer usually select the best color, text style, layout and images for a particular project. They also review the design to ensure that there will be no error before it will be printed or published. They also incorporate all the changes that was recommended by their clients to better improve their final design.

Other Careers

Aside from the careers that are mentioned above, Marketing Research Analysts, Public Opinion Researches, Telemarketers, Media Planners and Buyers and Statisticians are also some of the expected careers in advertising.
