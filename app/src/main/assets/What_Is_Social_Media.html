How Social Media Started

Since social media is more on communication and interaction, it could have started way before the 1900 and the first form of communication is letters. In 550 BC, this is where the first postal service has been dated and since then, it became one of the successful forms of communication back then.

When 1972 came, the Telegraph has been made. A telegraph will help in making long distances messages be quickly and easily delivered and this is the one used when it comes to sending information or news because the messages are just short. When the telegraph was out of trend, the pneumatic post entered in 1865 and it made the delivery even faster.

When the 20th century came, technologies started to advance and social media is part of it. Scientists in the 1940s made a way to connect the computers and the people with each other which gave birth to the Internet. Varieties of internet forms are been created like CompuServe that was formed in the 1960s. Then, emails came along and in 1979, UseNet allowed the users to interact via virtual newsletters. As the 1980s came, home computers started to be a common technology which led to social media’s sophistication. Chat relays were used and it now becomes popular until the 1990s.

In 19997, the first ever recognized social media site has been used and it is the Six Degrees that allows the user to socialize and upload profiles and the blogging sites also emerged.

 

When blogging became a trend after it was introduced, social media provided a booming effect and its popularity created a big storm. LinkedIn and Myspace began to exist, Flickr and Photobucket were also making a name in the world of photo sharing.

In 2005, YouTube, the popular video sharing, and the watching site also began. This site enables the user to share and communicate with other users in all parts of the globe.

In 2006, Twitter and Facebook suddenly popped out on the scene and has been captivating a lot of users since then. These sites remained its popularity until now and become the best social networking sites that can be found on the internet.

Now, you can find Spotify, Instagram, Tumblr, Foursquare, and Pinterest. These sites are also making names in the history of social media.

Social media really made a great history in the world today and it is one of the histories that we are still living today.
