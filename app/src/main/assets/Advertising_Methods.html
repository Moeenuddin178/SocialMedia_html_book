WHAT IS ADVERTISING? DIFFERENCE OF ADVERTISING IN MARKETING

Advertisement and marketing are often acknowledged the same. Marketing and advertising are very crucial aspects of any business; however, they are different from each other. One should know that very small line drawn between marketing and advertising. The knowledge regarding the difference between marketing and advertising helps the business to grow to extreme heights.

What is Advertising?

In case you didn’t know yet, advertisement is the most sought after and common way of popularizing a certain product or service. With advertising, the manufacturer attempts to showcase the positive aspects of an item, and how it’s advantageous for the consumer. In simple words, we can tell that advertising is the art of selling its services and products. Further, an advertising method must contain particular features, which help in its promotion, and these include:

  • Advertise the unique aspects of the product
  • It must be easily reached by the audience
  • The advertisement of the product must be a unique one
  • It has a well-defined market for the product.

Such features not just aid in good advertising, but it also guarantees the acceptance of the product within the market for a long period.

What is Marketing?

On the other hand, marketing is a more technical word than advertising, which includes more control and planning over several aspects. The marketing manager works in collaboration along with product and research manager at the best available market and buyers for the product. The manager selects the best potential market for the service or product that includes retailers, wholesalers, general consumers or government. In a nutshell, marketing talks about the art of making a demand for a certain item in different markets. Moreover, marketing also talks about pricing of the product for making the most of the profits together with offering customer satisfaction.

Difference Between Marketing and Advertising

As mentioned earlier, people sometimes ignore the slight difference between marketing and advertising. Thus, both marketing and advertising are normally utilized for one another. However, both marketing and advertising are different in several aspects. Some of their difference includes:

  • Different processes: Marketing is known to be a planned process where everything is controlled, planned and implemented, while advertising works on the fast approach of promoting a certain product via hoardings, the internet, and newspapers.
  • Component or Marketing Process: When we a get a wider look at both terms, then we know that marketing and advertising aren’t the same. Instead, advertising is a crucial component of the entire marketing process.

Advertising does have particular rules, which can be followed and if followed will help tip the odds in favor of a thriving campaign. Every effort at advertising must include the following in one form or another. Such principles have worked for many years and have made lots of services and products national brands.

Demonstrate

Showing your prospect how the item works is a crucial step. By showing how simple your item is to use, you’re giving the customer a reason to purchase. Through demonstrating the item, you involve them and help to visualize themselves by using your product successfully.

Educate

Perhaps you have the best widget in the land. It performs each other widget out there. You developed it and manufacturer in your basement. Yet, you don’t have sales? But why? You should educate your prospect. Tell the audience what is it, how they can use it, and how well it is made. Give them all the details they need so they’re informed.

Advertising and marketing are somewhat subjective that it can be difficult to know what effective marketing or advertising looks like.

7.3. COMMON ADVERTISING MISTAKES AND HOW TO AVOID THEM

There are a lot of ways to market a product or service on the internet, which a lot of people never become great at any of them. This results in advertising mistakes, which can be avoided. Here are some of them:

  • Insufficient focus on one advertising method – This is a typical mistake you observe made by interne advertising newbies. They shift all over the place trying different forms of internet advertising to learn how to perform one method prior to taking on the second one. Thus, they don’t develop meaningful traffic to their site because they don’t stick with something long enough for it to be thriving. 
  • Not testing and tracking the results – Think of it, how can you know what’s working in your advertising methods if you are not keeping track of the results it’s presenting? You must continuously be testing and tracking results and making adjustments to enhance the ads you’re running. 
  • Not opting to paid advertising – Everybody prefers to do as nearly free or methods of advertising as they can. Even though this is particularly a great idea from a cost point of view, you must avoid paid advertising as it can cost you a lot. Later on, you need to find several paid advertising methods, which work for you. If you concentrate on pay per click advertising, banner advertising, ezine advertising, buying visitors and much more. Some paid advertising is a crucial part of having a balance and equal marketing mix. 
  • Having unreasonable expectations – The online world is a huge world and to expect on the ad to make a big difference in the success isn’t a reasonable expectation. You need to check watch your advertising in the overall scheme of stuff and know that there are many competitions online at the same time take time for you to accomplish results and making adjustments to enhance the ads you’re running.

Here are other common advertising mistakes you need to avoid.

  • Focusing on services or products – If you wish to get the attention of your potential customers, talk to their wants and needs. The primary concern of your prospects isn’t that you have been in the business for 25 years. Utilize your ads to determine at least one common issue of your potential customers as well as the benefit of utilizing your products or service. 
  • Advertising could be one of the quickest means and grow the business or it could be the quickest wants to ruin your business. Through the right ad, you can attract to the business and boost your profits. A wrong ad can lead you to bankruptcy right away. For you to grow your business, you should attract the attention of your potential clients, advertising could aid you to do so when you utilize it properly. Unluckily, a lot of small business owners spend thousands of money on advertising efforts, which only acquire minimal results. 
  • Making use of wrong words – Bear in mind, a word here is a phrase where you can change your response rate by 100%. If you spend cash on advertising, test a number of versions of the copy to determine the one, which works best. 
  • Having weak marketing message – Too often you hear ads and it will take some thought to determine what they are even promoting. Ensure your advertisement consist of 7 to 10-word description of whom your product or service you serve, as well as the issues you solve so people who hear or read your ad will know how you can help them.

Common PPC Advertising Mistakes

Sending searchers to the home page – If you set up an online ad campaign with Yahoo, Google or Bing, you make the decision on which page which prospects lands on after clicking on your ad. Setting up your business is different online than it’s at your physical store. If individual clicks on the PPC ad, they’re looking for something specific. Ensure you bring them to the exact service or product they need.

Making generic copy – Did you know that through PPC ads, you have limited space to get the message out? Each single word counts. Utilizing generic words like excellent, superior and quality are costing you money. While such statements are true to the business, you need to target what the customer searched for, as they most likely aren’t searching for the business details, but they like to know how your service can help them fix their issue.

Secure high placement with the ads to score – Every search engine makes use of the method for identifying the ad’s impression and position. For instance, Google makes use of a combination of ad relevancy and the bid price. You should bid properly and make use of tight keywords in the ads as well as the landing page to get a placement on the search engines. Did you know that it’s estimated that around 85% of traffic generated from PPC comes from the ads within the top 3 positions of the page?

One mistake small businesses make is when they get a campaign running, they stop and let the campaign run. That’s a mistake! To keep on top, you’ll like to test other campaigns, add some keywords in a current campaign and split test the ads. By optimizing, you’ll also find keywords, which aren’t working anymore, at the same time cost you money with no results.
