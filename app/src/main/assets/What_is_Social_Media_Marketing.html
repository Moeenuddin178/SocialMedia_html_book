Chapter 3

WHAT IS SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING?

Social media is important. It does not only make communication effective, but it can make everything easy because of its different channels that can be found. Some people may say that it is only a waste of time but for some, social media plays a huge role in their lives – personal or business aspects.

Social media is mainly used for interactions between people but now, it is also being utilized for business purposes. Because of its great effectivity in helping a person gain new friends or share their thoughts and also in helping businesses to have a smooth marketing, social media will never be out of everyone’s life.

Why is it Important?

Social media, you may see it as a mainstream in the world of internet, is playing a big importance that you should never ignore. Without it, communication may have barriers which will stop you from conveying from what you want to tell. Its importance is a big game changer and if you miss this out, you might regret it in the end.

Social media can increase online presence

As we all know, social media is one way of making your exposure in the online scene become possible. Since there are several communication channels that can be found in social media, it will let your online presence to greatly improve and established. Facebook has billions of users around the world as well as Twitter and using these platforms, you will be able to reach your target audience and will also give you the chance to increase awareness.

Social media can grow customer base

If you are someone who is in the business world and would like to acquire more customers or grow your customer base, then social media is the answer. This becomes important because social media can widely increase the customer base by just sharing interesting contents, it can now catch the attention of the audience and will also have the chance to build more relationships or contacts.

Social media can market your business

Social media can help in marketing your business. Using this as a platform for marketing, you can easily appeal to the audience especially the targeted ones. Marketing your products or services through social media, you will have the opportunity to reach different people around the world since social media is being used worldwide.

Social media makes communication fast and easy

Social media is important because it can make communication fast and easy and this is what the people want. Nobody wants their messages to be delayed or will not have the chance to deliver what they want but with social media, communication will never be a problem. This is one of the reasons why this technology existed and it is because it wants to make communication be abrupt.

Social media is indeed important. Without it, communication may become hard and marketing may also become a hassle. So, if you ignore social media, you might be missing a big part of your life which you might regret in the end.

With social media, you can easily interact with others and will have the chance to make your business grow. Letting this slide out of your hands may not give you the result that you wanted. So, make use of social media, recognize its importance because it will be your way to a successful communication and marketing in the future.
