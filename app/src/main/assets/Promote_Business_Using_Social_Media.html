PROMOTE BUSINESS USING SOCIAL MEDIA

In the world of business today, social media has been an inexpensive tool that helps thousands of businesses in promoting their products, services, and websites to drive traffic and attract more prospective leads. You should leverage the power of social media if you want to grow your business today’s modern market.

The following chapters will guide you on how to promote a business using social media and the things that you need to know regarding the use of social media in a business setup. It is not easy to maintain a strong presence on social media but if you are guided by effective tips, the payoff will surely be incredible.

5.1. TOP SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORMS TO PROMOTE YOUR BUSINESS

Using social media is highly suggested to increase brand presence online. Nowadays, if you want your business to be recognized well, you have to be active on every social media platforms. While this is true to many, if you already have a social media manager, it will not be time-consuming for you to remain active on all social media platforms. Here are a few social media platforms that you can rule over to promote your business:

  • Twitter – Twitter is considered as a dominant democracy in the economy of social-sharing. The major keys to building a presence on Twitter are personality, brevity, and relevance. This platform is open for everyone.
  • Instagram – Instagram invites all brands that have visual content to be closer to their customers. This is for businesses related to food, lifestyle, personalities, fashion, and luxury brands. You can share short videos as visual content.
  • Facebook – Facebook is well-equipped when it comes to linearly sharing responses to posts asking questions or generating conversations. Any type of online ads, events, and content are allowed. It offers a personal connection for everyone.
  • YouTube – YouTube is the largest and most popular social media platform for video content and ads sharing as well as explainer videos and other videos that promote a brand, business, personality, etc. YouTube videos are prominently featured in Google search engine results.
  • Pinterest – Pinterest is different from other widely used search engines when it comes to recent content. Users simply pin and re-pin their posts to the Pinterest Boards, naturally pushing the content into Pinterest categories to categorize the content which is most appropriate for sharing easily and through keywords as well.
  • Yelp – With Yelp you can share the details regarding your business on a company profile page. You can monitor customer feedback and respond to their concerns in reviews. This is a free advertisement and promotion. Make sure to keep information updated and consider SEO.
  • Snapchat – Snapchat is another visual and mobile-only social media platform that is popular for its disappearing content. Posts are temporary and disappear within 24hrs. There is less pressure for you in creating a well-polished content. You can see the number of people who viewed your stories. This is ideal for small businesses.
  • LinkedIn – LinkedIn is helpful for you if you are in the B2B field since you can focus on connecting with other business professionals in different industries. This social network focuses on relationship building where you can easily target people by job title, industry, etc.

If you have not been active on social media lately, well, now is the perfect time to stay updated for your business! Through the social media platforms above, you can easily build the online presence of your brand in a cost-effective way. Moreover, you have a lot of options to choose from. Just pick the platform(s) that you believe will efficiently work for you and you are all set.

5.2. WAYS TO PROMOTE THE PRODUCTS OF YOUR BUSINESS USING SOCIAL MEDIA

Aiming for higher sales from social media sites? Looking for new strategies to share and promote the products of your business online? Well, you can definitely take advantage of the new features of Facebook, Snapchat, Pinterest, Instagram, etc. to get your products popular on social media and be seen by many users.

If you have no idea on how to get started, this chapter can surely guide through the process. There is no need for you to worry about not being savvy in using social media because in this chapter, rest assured that you will gain more understanding of how social media works to promote the products that you offer.

Here are the ways to promote the products of your business using social media that you can do:

Add links to the products in your Instagram Stories

Instagram feeds are now arranged according to popularity instead of a chronological order. That is why it is a challenging time for your business to be visible. Post Instagram Stories to be in front of your audience. Instagram allows you to add mentions and links in your posts so that it will be easier for your followers to browse and buy your products.

Import your online shop to your Facebook page

Business pages on Facebook is a great way for you to connect with your target audience to promote events and products. You can now import and feature your online store on your Facebook page directly by simply using Facebook plugins.

Display your products on Pinterest Showcase

One of the best things that Pinterest offers is its Showcase feature. Pinterest Showcase serves as a virtual storefront that is ideal for businesses to put their products on display. This is a rotating gallery that can be updated easily. You can feature themed boards in your Pinterest Showcase every week or every month.

Get in touch with customers personally on Snapchat

Snapchat ads appear between stories of friends and these are reserved for a lot of popular brands with big advertising budgets. But for businesses without the resources or the brand recognition strategies like advertising, the Snapchat stories can serve as their free and strategic channel to promote their business online. Moreover, you do not need to create well-polished posts since stories disappear within 24hrs.

Follow the 1-in-7 rule

If you have more than two social media accounts, apply the 1-in-7 rule on each as you promote your business. This means that only one of your seven posts daily shall overtly promote your business. The other six posts should be focused on valuable content which includes posts coming from communities. You can also promote your business on other posts by pairing it with a relevant content. Always include relevant content along with your promotion to keep your audience engaged.

Ask conversation-starter questions

Social media users enjoy conversations and sharing opinions. So, if you have a Facebook page, expect that your followers and other users will be more interested in relevant topics about your business. Make it a point to engage them by asking questions to start conversations. Your questions should engage and inspire them to choose your business.

Include behind the scenes videos or influencer videos

Visual content plays a vital role in social media marketing. Not only that well-captured photographs, ads, etc. are great to attract customers but most especially engaging video content. You can include YouTube video links on your Facebook posts, Instagram Stories or tweets for customers to have a bigger view of what you do in your business. User-generated content or influencer videos are very powerful as well as behind the scenes of how you produce your products and deliver your services.

5.3. PROS AND CONS OF SOCIAL MEDIA AS A PROMOTIONAL TOOL

Social media has significantly grown from being only a mere channel for socializing digitally and has become one of the most powerful and effective marketing tools that businesses and brands are using. However, if you are planning to promote your business using social media, you should also be aware of the pros and cons of it to be more careful and strategic in maximizing your benefits using social media.

Here are the pros and cons of social media as a promotional tool:

PROS

  • It is cheaper than other marketing tools

Almost all social media networks are free for everyone to use. It is practically cheaper compared to other marketing tools since you create your own business profile easily on different platforms as well as post relevant content regularly without spending a cent.

  • It exposes your brand to a larger audience

Now that we are already in a digital era, almost every person that you meet has a social media account or has the idea of using social media. This only means that your audience is not limited to a certain number.

  • It builds authentic relationships

Social media is one of your channels to build a personal relationship with your customers. You can easily connect with them on Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, Twitter, YouTube, etc. Moreover, it induces brand loyalty, has higher conversion rate, and provides marketplace insights.

CONS

  • Time-extensive

You have to dedicate a lot of your time to maintain an interactive presence online.

  • Prone to security breaches

Since a lot of people have access to social media, it is not impossible to encounter security breaches, scams, and fraud. Hackers are everywhere and can just break into accounts.

  • Brand image may be damaged permanently

Anything you post is public and will be directly held against your business.

  • ROI is hard to measure

Social media is not perfect. It is not easy to measure your gains unless your business is really a hit.

Use all social media platforms

As much as possible, if you can, utilize all platforms for maximum visibility. You may choose one or two but it is better to leverage as many as you can. You do not only gain more audience but you also improve your strategy on each platform.

Format your content to optimize for each platform

SEO is very important to attract more leads and gain higher conversions. Make sure that your content is engaging, relevant, and optimized for people to easily find you and choose your products and services.

Push on platforms that work well

As mentioned, you can use all platforms so that you can learn each platform’s strengths and weaknesses. Thus, you will know which platform works well with your campaigns.

Make sure your content aligns with your message

Pair a relevant content with the message that your business wants to share to your audience. Keep them interested by being consistent with what you post.

Sign up for HootSuite among other social media managers out there. It provides you a way to manage all accounts on a single site, scheduling your messages and be able to review and analyze brand feedback. Check it out.

Be sure update posts. Before you add a friend on your business account, make sure you have linked contents, stories, photos, and information about your industry so as these “friends” would find something interesting to look at your page and share with their friends.

For example on YouTube, you could provide informative videos about your business, satisfied customer experiences and can encourage other customers to create their own experience video. Be sure to share those video to your other accounts as well.

Find Followers and Friends. Searching keywords can help you out when looking for followers. Look for them on other people’s site and maybe you can try commenting and sharing their post as well, creating a sort of trustworthy relationship and might do the same for you. Look for famous influencers, like bloggers, media outlets, and maybe city officers as well. Hope for the best and you might find yourself with a thousand followers.

Connect with Friends. It might seem awkward, but in some cases, some followers tend to turn to be great friends when you get to know them. Some might even be strangers at first commenting on how he or she was impressed with how your business grew over the few months, then next thing you know you find yourself impressed with your new found friend’s business establishment. So it is a win-win situation to get to know one of your regular critics or avid followers.

So in all in all, the most vital thing to do when promoting your business through social media is to start and build that connecting relationship with your people.

5.5. BEST SOCIAL MEDIA STRATEGIES FOR A SUCCESSFUL BUSINESS PROMOTION

Using the Social Media as a portal for business promotion is very familiar and popular among the people nowadays. Aside from convenience, it offers the customer a chance to look for more just by browsing. And I think you practice yourself reading more.

Various social media accounts such as Google, Facebook, Twitter and many others have become one of the alternative solutions in marketing processes. You could be successful if you manage to work your marketing strategy interesting and better than the other.

It does not necessarily mean that you just need to focus on the strategy, there are some factors too that need to be considered in order for you to successfully establish a name in social media. Some of the helpful social media strategies that you can apply in order for your business promotion to be effective are the following. Just follow this idea and for sure, you will have a better business promotion in Social Media.

EVALUATE YOUR BUSINESS

This is the initial step that should be done. You should first look for the possible idea of a business that you want to engage in social media. Assess everything. The business itself, the assets, the target customers and even the idea on how you will gain the attention of the people in the media. You should intelligently and diligently expose the business promotions.

CREATE SOCIAL MEDIA ACCOUNT

You can create any account in Facebook, Google, YouTube, Twitter and other common social media accounts and even create your own business page where you can advertise all =the business promotions. It will be the most convenient and easy way to manage the promotional advertisement. It is advisable that you look for a social media account that is popular and at the same time accessible to the large audience.

CHOOSE A SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGER

Having multiple accounts in social media is hassle and time-consuming. It would be nice if you first sign up for a specific media account that will serve as the manager of all the social media accounts you have created. With this, you can easily manage the accounts just by using a single site and even create messages to be deployed in the different social media accounts.  In addition, you can use statistics regarding with the promotional flow of the business. You need not worry in the dissemination of the business promotion in the media accounts.

RESEARCH THE COMPETITOR’S BUSINESS PROMOTION

Researching for the business competitor’s promotion will definitely be helpful in your part because you can acquire some of the ideas that they integrated into the promotions. By just reading their promotions, you will eventually learn how they run the promotions through social media and this will be the best hint for you to employ the ideas that would be helpful in your part. Analyze the content strategy they used, check for the number of followers or fan and the posting frequency, the likes and shares and even comments. You should study everything about your competitor’s way of attracting an audience.

CREATE AN EFFECTIVE CONTENT STRATEGY

Creating a content that will catch the audience interest is a little problematic. The content must be meaningful and understandable in order for the business promotion responsive in the eyes of the prospective clients. You can use any form of content strategy by means of text or even videos and messages that are highly focused on what the promotion is all about.

Business promotion by means of various social media accounts may be effective tools in spreading the word of your business. You just need to carefully analyze, study and apply necessary information essential for the business promotion to click in the audience.

Social media feedback will determine the condition of your product in the audience. It may be a double edge sword in your part; the one indicates for your responsiveness in building brand and gaining customer loyalty while the other is for the restraint against the negative comments in your brand.

People in social media always have the right to voice out their sights on a particular product or situation. Reduce Marketing Cost

The communications between customers, the promotional offers, the products sales and announcement, surveys and customer feedback is the many marketing strategies that are traditionally run by money. Through the use of social media, you can easily employ this strategy via social media. You now have a chance to leverage all the possible expenses generated by the old way of retail business.

Social Media became the initiator of change in almost all the industry of the world. It redefines the business environment and the communication between the retailer and the consumer. Well, it is not surprising to be like this, since we are in an environment that could easily change. Retailing is considered as a social activity and through social media, it even becomes useful in marketing and retailing of the brands.

As the long run of business dominates the society, the social media modifies the traditional retail business which eventually transforms it into a more convenient and effective one. Just follow all the essential factors in order for your business to be a success in social media. You have the idea and you must work for it to be more successful.
