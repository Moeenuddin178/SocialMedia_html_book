What is social media?

The first part of this book would be discussing what social media is. Breaking down the term, “social” means interacting with other people. It may be our family, friends or even with strangers whom we haven’t even met yet in person. It can also be the process of exchanging information with one another. On the other hand, media is a communication instrument. An example of this would be the newspapers, TV and even the radio. So, when we combine those two terms together, it basically means a communication tool online that allows people to share information and interact with each other.

 Advertising methods

There are different methods that you can use in advertising on social media. One of those methods is the PPC or the Pay Per Click. It is a method of advertising wherein the advertiser would only be paid when someone clicks on the ad. So, it is very important for you to ensure that the content of your ads would have a high click rate. Another method that you can try is the PPV or the Pay Per view, where the amount charged depends on the number of views that an ad has gotten. It is often charged in groups of views.
